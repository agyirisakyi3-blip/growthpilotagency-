# Marketing Playbook: LinkedIn Outreach, Guest Posting & Cold Email

## 1. LinkedIn Outreach

**Target**: Founders, CEOs, Heads of Marketing at companies with 10–200 employees, poor Core Web Vitals, or no structured data.

**Profile Setup**:
- Use the GEO Meta Tag Generator as a free lead magnet ("I built a free tool that checks if your site is AI-search ready")
- Post 3x/week: audit screenshots, GEO trends, client result snippets

**Outflow Sequence** (3 steps, 2 days apart):

1. **Connection request** (no note, or a 2-line note):
   > "Hey [Name] — I noticed [Company]'s site could benefit from GEO optimization. I built a free tool that might help."

2. **Value message** (after connection):
   > "Thanks for connecting! I ran [Company].com through our free audit tool and found [1–2 specific issues, e.g. 'no FAQ schema and missing alt text on 5 images']. These are easy fixes that help you show up in AI search results. Happy to share a quick 3-point action plan if you're interested."

3. **Soft CTA** (2 days later):
   > "No pressure at all — if you'd like a full 15-min audit of your site's AI search readiness, I'm happy to jump on a quick call this week. Otherwise, the free tool is always available at growthpilotagency.com/audit."

**Targeting criteria** (LinkedIn Sales Navigator):
- Industry: E-commerce, SaaS, Professional Services, Healthcare, Real Estate
- Company size: 11–200
- Posts about: SEO, marketing, growth, AI
- Geography: US/UK/Canada (or your priority cities)

## 2. Guest Posting

**Goal**: 2–3 high-authority backlinks/month from sites your target audience reads.

**Target sites by tier**:

| Tier | Sites | Pitch angle |
|------|-------|-------------|
| A | Search Engine Journal, Search Engine Land, Moz Blog | "How GEO is replacing traditional SEO: a framework for 2026" |
| B | SaaS blogs (Shopify, HubSpot, Webflow, Stripe), Indie Hackers | "5 AI search optimizations that doubled our organic traffic in 90 days" |
| C | Niche industry blogs | "Local GEO: how [industry] businesses can dominate AI search" |

**Pitch template**:

> Subject: Article idea: [Angle relevant to their audience]
>
> Hi [Editor],
>
> I'm a contributor at GrowthPilot Agency, where we specialize in GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) — helping businesses show up in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.
>
> I've noticed [Target Publication] covers [their recent relevant article], but I think your readers would benefit from a practical guide on [your angle]. I've seen firsthand how [stat/fact about results].
>
> Here's what I'm thinking:
> - **[Proposed title]**
> - Key insight / framework they haven't covered
> - Real data from case studies
> - Actionable takeaways
>
> I've written for [any past publications if you have them, otherwise skip]. Would this be a fit for [Publication]?

**Outreach workflow**:
1. Find editor's email using hunter.io or by checking the publication's "Write for Us" page
2. Send pitch
3. Follow up in 7 days
4. If accepted, deliver draft within 10 days
5. Include 1 contextual link back to your audit tool or meta tag generator

## 3. Cold Email

**Target**: Companies ranking on page 2–3 for their primary keyword (they're close but not quite there — GEO can push them over).

**Tooling**: Apollo.io or Instantly for leads + sending. 30–50 emails/day to start.

**Sequence** (4 emails, 3 days apart):

**Email 1 — Audit hook**:
> Subject: [Company] + AI search
>
> Hi [First],
>
> Quick question: are you tracking how your site shows up in ChatGPT or Google AI Overviews?
>
> I ran [Company].com through our free GEO audit and found it's missing [1 specific thing, e.g. "FAQ schema that would help you appear in AI answers"].
>
> This is a 5-minute fix that can put you in front of users who never click traditional search results.
>
> If you want, I can send over a 3-point checklist specific to your site — no strings attached.

**Email 2 — Value add** (if no reply):
> Subject: Re: [Company] + AI search
>
> [First],
>
> Just wanted to share a quick resource we put together — it's a free GEO Meta Tag Generator that creates the exact schema and tags AI search engines look for:
>
> [link to tool]
>
> It generates everything in one click. Might be useful for your team.

**Email 3 — Case study** (3 days later):
> Subject: How [similar company] increased AI visibility by 340%
>
> [First],
>
> We recently worked with a [industry] company similar to yours. By adding structured data + optimizing for GEO, they went from appearing in 0 to 12 AI-generated answers for their target keywords in 6 weeks.
>
> Happy to share the specific changes we made if you're interested.

**Email 4 — Breakup** (5 days later):
> Subject: Closing the loop
>
> [First],
>
> No worries if the timing isn't right. Our free audit tool is always available if you want to check your site's GEO readiness later:
>
> [link to audit]
>
> Take care,
> [Your name]

**Quick wins checklist** to include in email 1:
- [ ] Add FAQ schema (boosts AI answer appearance by ~40%)
- [ ] Add Open Graph + Twitter Card tags
- [ ] Ensure meta description is 120–160 chars
- [ ] Add a canonical URL
- [ ] Compress images and enable Gzip/Brotli
- [ ] Add one H1 per page
